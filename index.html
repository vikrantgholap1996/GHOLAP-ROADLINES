About GHOLAP ROADLINES

GHOLAP ROADLINES is a trusted name in the transportation industry, specializing in the safe and timely transport of molasses. With over 20 years of experience, we have been serving sugar factories, distilleries, and processing units across Maharashtra and neighboring states.

Our commitment to punctuality, safety, and transparency makes us one of the most reliable logistics partners in the region. We operate a robust fleet of well-maintained tankers and are supported by a dedicated team of professionals.
